About Me

Hi! I'm Khelan Reddy but I go by the alias Robin, a 2D digital artist and game designer. I love creating vibrant visuals, game concepts, and digital experiences.

Ever since I was young I always loved being creative drawing and listening/creating music was one of my favourite passtimes when I didn't feel like playing video games. I get satisfaction from creating media that people are able to enjoy but art has become something that becomes fulfilling to me, where I can easily express myself and challenge myself to become a better artist.

💼 What I Do

  • Create 2D illustrations, characters & game assets
  • Design UI & concept art for games
  • Explore and build mini-games
  • Dabble in web design & development

🎓 Education

Bachelor of Arts in Game Design & Production
SAE Institute Johannesburg

🎮 Interests

  • Playing video games
  • Reading & drawing
  • Streaming
  • Music – Click Me

My goal is to work in the gaming industry as an artist, whether on concept art or illustration. I want to be the reason, like artists have done for me, that someone picks up a pen and starts creating!
